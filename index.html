How to Play

Goal

Guess the hidden material. After each guess, you'll receive color-coded feedback comparing your guess to the target material across 10 properties.

Feedback Colors

8
Green — Exact match on this property
↓7
Yellow — Close! Within 1 point of target
↓9
Red ↓ — Your guess is too HIGH
↑3
Blue ↑ — Your guess is too LOW
—
Gray — Wrong category

Properties

  • Category — Plant, Animal, Metal, Mineral, Synthetic
  • Hardness — Resistance to scratching (1=butter, 10=diamond)
  • Flexibility — Bends without breaking
  • Density — Weight per unit volume
  • Water Resistance — Repels water
  • Conductivity — Conducts electricity
  • Insulation — Thermal insulation ability
  • Transparency — How see-through it is
  • Durability — Long-term wear resistance
  • Cost — Relative material cost

Tools

  • 💡 Hint — Reveals one correct property of the target
  • 👁 Reveal — Shows the answer and starts over
  • ≡ Encyclopedia — Browse all materials and their properties
  • Radar chart — Shows your last guess and all confirmed correct values in green
